How partners are ranked
Each partner is scored out of 100 points across 6 dimensions. Two dimensions — Stalled Pipeline and Discontinuation — are inverse scores. All metrics show Q3 vs Q2 deltas to surface momentum, not just snapshot position. Partners scoring 75+ Strong · 55–74 Moderate · 35–54 Watch · <35 At-Risk
