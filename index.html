Platform Logic

One intelligence loop,
four connected layers.

Each module operates independently but feeds the next. Market signals become campaigns. Campaigns generate deals. Deals inform partner performance.

01 · SIGNAL
Detect & Prioritise
SignalOps identifies accounts showing expansion signals, funding events, and ERP fit. A multi-dimensional TAL scoring model surfaces the right accounts at the right moment.
02 · ACTIVATE
Build & Launch
CampaignForge converts prioritised signals into targeted GTM programs. AI-generated personas, messaging, and channel strategy — ready to execute.
03 · MANAGE
Score & Forecast
DealLens monitors every deal in the pipeline for risk signals, engagement gaps, and forecast confidence — with recommended actions for each seller.
04 · SCALE
Track & Optimise
PartnerPulse measures partner pipeline contribution, performance trends, and QBR readiness — closing the loop from signal to ecosystem impact.
