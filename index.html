Platform Logic

One intelligence loop,
four connected layers.

Each module operates independently but feeds the next. Market signals become campaigns. Campaigns generate deals. Deals inform partner performance.

01 · SIGNAL
Detect & Prioritise
SignalOps identifies accounts showing expansion signals, funding events, and ERP fit. A multi-dimensional TAL scoring model surfaces the right accounts at the right moment.
02 · ACTIVATE
Build & Launch
CampaignForge converts prioritised signals into targeted GTM programs. AI-generated personas, messaging, and channel strategy — ready to execute.
03 · MANAGE
Score & Forecast
DealLens monitors every deal in the pipeline for risk signals, engagement gaps, and forecast confidence — with recommended actions for each seller.
04 · SCALE
Track & Optimise
PartnerPulse measures partner pipeline contribution, performance trends, and QBR readiness — closing the loop from signal to ecosystem impact.

Think Tank · New Age Marketing Custodian of Analytics Proponent of Partner Evolution Systems Builder

I build systems. Not because the tools are interesting — though they are — but because a well-designed system changes how people make decisions. The four modules in this platform exist because I kept running into the same gap: good data, no intelligence layer on top of it.

I think of myself as a think tank for new age marketing. Not a campaign executor, but someone who sits at the intersection of signal, strategy, and execution — asking what the data is actually saying before anyone decides what to do next.

On analytics, I believe a custodian mindset matters more than a reporting mindset. Numbers without context are noise. My job is to turn noise into a point of view that a sales leader, an MU head, or a regional DCM can act on in the next 24 hours.

On partners, I think the ecosystem is underutilised everywhere. Partner intelligence is the last mile most revenue functions haven’t cracked. PartnerPulse is my attempt to start that conversation.
