Corporate ERP Intelligence · Dual-Track Inflection Model
The Corporate ERP Signal Score evaluates accounts across seven dimensions and assigns a TAL tier. Tier 1 (75+) = monthly enrichment and full seller activation. Tier 2 (55-74) = quarterly enrichment and relationship-building. Tier 3 (35-54) = monitor. Out of TAL (<35) = deprioritise. Dimension 2 uses a dual-track inflection model: funded companies score on stage maturity; bootstrapped and family-owned businesses score on expansion, leadership transition, regulatory pressure, and hiring momentum - signals with equivalent GTM urgency.
