Risk Score Methodology · How DealLens Calculates Deal Risk
Scoring Methodology Note
The penalty weights in this model are heuristic, derived from observed patterns across enterprise B2B SaaS sales cycles rather than statistical regression on closed-won/lost data. The recommended validation path is to instrument conversion rates by risk band across a deal cohort of 100+ opportunities, then apply logistic regression to derive empirically-weighted coefficients. A/B testing sales interventions triggered at specific risk thresholds would provide the first ground-truth dataset for calibration.
